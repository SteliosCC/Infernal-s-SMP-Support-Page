Welcome to the Economy page!


>What is Economy?

-Economy is a virtual currency used by the Infernal SMP®

>What can I do with the Money?

-You can use your money by interacting with /shop and click the category you want to buy items from or in /ah to buy items from other Players.

>How do I get COINS?

-You can do /withdraw %amount% to convertmoney into COINS which is the currency the Villager in Spawn ( Crate keys , Tags and Black Market Villages)


>How do I get Money?

•There are some ways to earn money!

    1) Selling Items in /shop.

    2) Killing Mobs has a 90% Chance to drop from 3$ all the Way to 1000$ , depens on the Mob. (VIP : x3 multiplier & Member+ x2 multiplier)

    3) By using the command /reward
