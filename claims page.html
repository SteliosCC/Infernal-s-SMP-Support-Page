Welcome to the Claims Help page!


>What are CLAIMS and how do I use them?

-You can use the command /c claim to claim a chuck,but be carefull,the chucks aren't claimed yet!

-Use /c recipe to see the recipe.Place down a chest and put the recipe in the center inside your claimed chuck.

-Use /c show to see the aera your chest is claiming.

-Above the chest you can see the Time you have left of claiming this chuck!To get more Time add Iron/Diamond or click the Coin icon to add Coins.

-Use the Icons at the bottom of the chest to manage your claimed aera settings.

-Use /c invite %player_name% to invite them to your aera. Use /c accept to join to become Member of that claim

-Use /c name to rename your claimed chucks.

-Note : The more Chucks you have claimed the higher the price is!
